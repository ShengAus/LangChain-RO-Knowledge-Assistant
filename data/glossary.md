# RO Glossary

Recovery: The percentage of feed flow converted into permeate.

Permeate: The treated water that passes through the membrane.

Concentrate: The reject stream that retains a higher solute concentration.

Flux: Permeate production normalized by membrane area, commonly reported in LMH.

Pretreatment: Upstream processing used to reduce fouling, scaling, and particulate loading before the RO unit.
